FAQ

How do I reset my game?
Open the game and start a New Game from the pause menu.

Leaderboard not updating?
Make sure you are signed in to Game Center, finish a run, and allow a short time for scores to appear.

Troubleshooting

  1. Update iOS and the app to the latest version.
  2. Force-quit and reopen the app.
  3. Restart your device.

If the issue persists, email me with your device model, iOS version, and a short description of the problem.
