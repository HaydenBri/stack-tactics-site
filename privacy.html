Stack Tactics – Privacy Policy

Effective date: 29 August 2025
Developer: Hayden Brierley

Overview

Stack Tactics is a single-player puzzle game. I do not collect, store, or sell personal information. Scores and game state are saved locally on your device.

Game Center

If you choose to use Apple Game Center for leaderboards, Apple may process information such as your Game Center display name, player identifier, and scores. This data is handled by Apple under Apple’s privacy policy. I do not receive your personal details from Apple. See Apple’s Privacy Policy.

Crash logs and diagnostics

If you share diagnostics with developers in iOS settings, Apple may provide anonymised crash information. I use this only to fix bugs. No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs are used.

Children

Game Center requires an eligible Apple ID. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data storage and retention

Game progress and settings are stored on your device (UserDefaults). Deleting the app removes this data.
